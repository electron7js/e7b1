Smores

Tags:Easy, Quick, Chocolate
Easy to make, 5 minutes preparation
Ingredients
  • 1 Regular Marshmallow
  • 1 Graham cracker sheet
  • One square of milk Chocolate
Methods
  • Step 1. Heat a grill to medium heat, or light a fire, or get an oven on low heat
  • Step 2. Split the Graham cracker into 2 equal squares
  • Step 3. Take a square cracker piece and place a Chocolate square on it
  • Step 4. Place a Marshmallow on top of the Chocolate square
  • Step 5. Place the other square cracker piece on top and gently push it downs
  • Step 6. Put the whole thing into the heat source(oven,grill,fire,etc) at a place of gentle heat and heat it till the Marshmallow and chocolate melt
  • Step 7. Take the smore out carefully and serve.
