Garlic Bread

Tags:Bread, Easy
Easy to make, 15 minutes preparation
Ingredients
  • 1 French bread (regular bread works too)
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 100g butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parsely leaves
  • Parmessan or any other cheese to taste
Methods
  • Step 1. Peel the garlic and mince it
  • Step 2. Gently heat the butter until it softens, then mix in the minced garlic
  • Step 3. Add salt and pepper to the mix as per taste
  • Step 4. Slice the bread to desirable slices
  • Step 5. Apply the mixed paste as if buttering toast
  • Step 6. Add parsely leaves on top
  • (optional) Grate some cheese on the bread
  • Step 7.Put the slices in the oven at 210C and let it cook for 10minutes
